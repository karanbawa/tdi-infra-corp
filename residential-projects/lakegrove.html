Project Description

Nicely perched in green natural surroundings, TDI Lake Grove is a township built around an exclusive 4-acre lake. Designed artistically, and master planned by world's leading architectural firm, HOK, London, this world-class township endows lavish lifestyle handpicked just for you.


TDI Lake Grove is expediently situated at a heart skip distance from North Delhi, and with rising infrastructures like Hospitals, Education centres and Recreational amenities in Kundli, you will get everything you wanted as a part of your holistic living experience. Now, cherish the freedom of an exquisite lifestyle without compromising on the distance, as it touches Delhi's border along NH-1 through junction of 30 metre and 45 metre Sector Road, exclusively leading to TDI Lake Grove. Also, it's a perfect avenue for other states like Punjab, Haryana and Himachal Pradesh.


With improving infrastructure and upcoming fast connectivity, Kundli-Sonepat is emerging as the new hotspot for end-users and buyers. The 135 km long Kundli-Manesar-Palwal (KMP) Expressway could make the real estate projects on Kundli-Sonepat zone more viable for investors.


To plan, design and create the elevations of TDI Lake Grove, TDI has partnered with world's leading global design, architecture, engineering and planning firm, HOK.
