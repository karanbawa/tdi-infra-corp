THE GRAND RETREAT

TDI The Grand Retreat is a residential and commercial development of TDI Infracorp. Located in the outskirts of Sector 88, Faridabad, it is built across a wide area. These luxurious 3 BHK, 4 BHK floors, residential plots, commercial shops and office space make it a dream home to everyone. These apartments are beautifully crafted with modern amenities.

The Grand Retreat is spread in 57 acres with 3 sides Open Township without any other land, high tension wires. This residential gated plot located at prime location of Greater Faridabad near to Amma hospital, Puri Amanvilas, RPS City and just 500m away from 75m wide road of 6 lanes. First Time TDI Group launched gated plot with 3-Tier Security and luxury amenities such as Swimming Pool, Central Park, 2 Acres of club in campus, 5 Acres high school in campus, 2 Commercials in campus of 1 acres and 1.25 acres, Dispensary of 1.25 acres in Campus, 0.20 Acres land for Temple, 3 Nursery Schools of 0.20 acres in campus, Cretch of 0.20 acres in Campus, One Nursing home of 0.20 acres. Located on one of the most convenient, hottest and most preferred location in Faridabad. Just a few minutes from Mathura Road. The location is adjacent to Retail, Shopping Centers.
